About MIAMI FLORIDA

Miami, located in southeastern Florida, is one of the most vibrant and diverse cities in the United States. Known for its beautiful beaches, sunny weather, and lively culture, Miami attracts millions of visitors each year. The city serves as a gateway to Latin America, offering a rich blend of cultures, especially Cuban, which has greatly influenced its food, music, and arts scene. Miami is also known for its modern skyline, luxurious shopping, and exciting nightlife.
