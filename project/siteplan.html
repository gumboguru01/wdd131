Murudo – Where Love Finds You

Site Plan Document by Nyasha Gumboguru

Site Name

Murudo — "Murudo" means "In Love" in Shona. This name represents a modern, intelligent dating experience rooted in culture and innovation. The name is short, memorable, emotionally resonant, and perfect for a global dating brand.
Optional domain availability: murudo.ai

Site Purpose

The Murudo website supports an AI-powered mobile dating app by offering:

  • Feature overviews like AI matchmaking, video profiles, blind chat mode, and location-based swiping
  • A blog for love confessions and stories
  • User help, safety, and contact pages
  • Web-based account creation and premium upgrade

Scenarios

  • How can I find a serious relationship using AI suggestions?
  • Is there a safe way to chat with strangers before showing my picture?
  • Can I read or share real love stories from others who’ve used the app?

Color Schema

  • #FF3366 (Rose Red): Used for headers, buttons, and accents that spark passion and romance.
  • #FFF5F8 (Blush White): Used for background, cards, and soft interface areas to keep a clean and warm look.
  • #222222 (Deep Gray): Used for primary text for readability and contrast.

Typography

  • 'Playfair Display' — Used for headings and the logo to give a romantic and elegant touch.
  • 'Open Sans' — Used for all body and paragraph text due to its simplicity and clarity.
